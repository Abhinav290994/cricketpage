IPL 2017 News

The 2017 season of the Indian Premier League, also known as IPL 10, is the tenth season of the IPL, a professional Twenty20 cricket league established by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007. The tournament features the eight teams that played in the previous season. The 2017 season started on 5 April 2017 and is scheduled to finish on 21 May 2017, with Hyderabad hosting the opening match and the final. Sunrisers Hyderabad are the defending champions having won the 2016 edition.
